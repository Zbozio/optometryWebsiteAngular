Services

Free eye test

Our specialised opticians are waiting for you in store, where you can take advantage of a free eye test without making an appointment. The service is always free and available, even on weekends.

Glasses ready in one hour

Each store is equipped with a grinding machine that is used to shape and adapt lenses to the type of alteration and treatment required. This means that most lenses can be made directly in store.

Offer available only in Italy

Glasses to your home

If lenses can’t be made directly in store, for example due to a very complex alteration, don’t worry: we’ll send the glasses to your home by courier without charging shipping fees.
